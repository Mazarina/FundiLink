# PROJECT_CONTEXT.md — FundiLink by ZulTek

## What Is FundiLink?

FundiLink is a South African student opportunity platform built by ZulTek. It gives learners — particularly those in rural and under-resourced communities — a single, intelligent profile that helps them navigate the full journey from NSC results to higher education, funding, skills development, and early career opportunities.

**One profile. Every opportunity.**

---

## Why ZulTek Is Building FundiLink

South Africa has a significant access problem. Thousands of qualifying learners do not make it to university, TVET college, bursaries, or skills programmes — not because they don't qualify, but because:

- The application process is fragmented across dozens of institutions and portals
- Learners in rural areas have limited access to guidance counsellors
- Many learners don't know what they qualify for
- Document preparation is complex, and mistakes cause rejections
- Deadlines are missed due to lack of awareness
- Many families don't have the digital literacy or access to navigate multiple systems

ZulTek is building FundiLink to close this gap using technology, good design, and a learner-first approach.

---

## How FundiLink Helps Rural Learners

FundiLink is specifically designed with the rural and under-resourced learner in mind:

- **Mobile-first design** — most rural learners access the internet via smartphone
- **WhatsApp integration (future)** — meet learners where they are
- **Plain language** — no jargon; clear, actionable guidance
- **One profile** — fill in information once; it works everywhere
- **APS calculator** — learners instantly know what they qualify for
- **Document checklist** — never miss a required document again
- **Deadline tracking** — notifications for application deadlines
- **School pilot programme** — partner with schools to bring FundiLink directly into the guidance counsellor workflow

## What the MVP Is

**FundiLink SmartApply** is the MVP. It covers:

1. Learner registration and education profile
2. APS calculation from captured NSC results
3. Programme matching (what universities/TVETs does this learner qualify for?)
4. Document checklist and vault (upload, organise, track)
5. Application tracker (what has the learner applied to? What is the status?)
6. Basic notifications (email + in-app)
7. Admin portal (support agents, platform admins)
8. School dashboard foundation (for pilot schools)

The MVP does **not** include:
- WhatsApp integration
- Automated submissions to institutions
- AI guidance assistant
- Bursary partner integrations
- Payment gateway
- Official NSFAS/university API integration (unless a formal partnership is in place)

## What the Long-Term Ecosystem Could Become

FundiLink has the potential to become a comprehensive South African student opportunity ecosystem:

- **FundiLink SmartApply** — university, TVET, and skills programme applications
- **FundiLink Bursary Hub** — bursary discovery, matching, and tracking
- **FundiLink Skills** — short courses, learnerships, and artisan pathways
- **FundiLink Housing** — student accommodation discovery and applications
- **FundiLink Mentorship** — connecting learners with mentors in their field
- **FundiLink Early Career** — graduate programmes, internships, and first jobs
- **FundiLink AI Guide** — intelligent, conversational guidance assistant
- **FundiLink for Schools** — full school counsellor platform
- **FundiLink for Partners** — institution and funder portal for direct integration

ZulTek's long-term vision is for FundiLink to be the platform that every South African learner uses from Grade 11 through to their first job.

---

## Important Positioning

FundiLink is **not** an official admissions portal for any government body, university, TVET college, NSFAS, or bursary programme unless a formal, documented partnership and integration exists.

FundiLink helps learners:
- Prepare their applications
- Understand their eligibility
- Organise their documents
- Track their applications
- Stay informed about deadlines

Actual submissions must go through official channels unless an official integration is in place.

This positioning must be maintained in all product copy, UI text, marketing materials, and communications.
