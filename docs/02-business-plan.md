# 02 — Business Plan

## Title
FundiLink by ZulTek — Business Plan Overview

## Purpose
Outline the business model, revenue strategy, and commercial viability of FundiLink.

---

## Company
**ZulTek** — South African technology company
**Product:** FundiLink (flagship product)
**Stage:** Pre-revenue, building MVP

## Problem and Market
See `/docs/01-product-vision.md` for the full problem statement.
Target market: South African Grade 11/12 learners and recent matriculants (~600,000+ per year), school guidance counsellors, and the institutions that want to reach them.

## Revenue Model

### Primary Revenue Streams (MVP and beyond)

| Stream | Description | Phase |
|---|---|---|
| Institutional listings | Institutions pay to have verified, enhanced programme listings | Phase 3+ |
| Bursary partner listings | Funders pay to reach matched, eligible learners | Phase 6 |
| School subscriptions | Schools pay for the counsellor dashboard and premium features | Phase 4 |
| Premium learner features | Optional paid features for learners (priority matching, CV builder) | Phase 4+ |
| Data insights (anonymised) | Aggregate trend reports for institutions and government (anonymised, POPIA-compliant) | Phase 6+ |
| White-label / API | Licence FundiLink technology to NGOs, corporates, or provincial education departments | Phase 7+ |

### MVP Revenue Strategy
MVP is freemium — learner-facing features are free. Institutional listings are free initially to build the programme database. Revenue focus begins in Phase 3/4.

## Cost Structure
- Development (ZulTek team + tools)
- Cloud hosting (API server, database, document storage)
- Email/notification costs
- Legal and compliance (POPIA, T&Cs)
- Marketing and school outreach

## Key Partners
- Schools and school districts (pilot programme)
- Universities and TVET colleges (data and eventual integrations)
- Bursary providers (data and eventual partnerships)
- DHET (Department of Higher Education and Training) — aspirational
- NGOs in education access space — potential channel partners

## Key Metrics to Track
- Registered learners
- Profiles completed (APS calculated)
- Applications tracked
- Documents uploaded
- School accounts active
- Learner-to-opportunity conversion (did they get in?)

## Next Actions

- Identify 5 pilot schools for Phase 4 school dashboard
- Define partnership approach for bursary providers
- Determine grant funding opportunities (e.g., SEDA, IDC, Harambee, education-focused foundations)
