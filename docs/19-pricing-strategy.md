# 19 — Pricing Strategy

## Title
FundiLink — Pricing Strategy

## Purpose
Define the pricing model for FundiLink's products and services across all customer segments.

---

## Pricing Principles

1. **Learner access must always be affordable or free** — never price out the learner we exist to serve
2. **Revenue comes from the system that benefits from learner access** — institutions, funders, schools
3. **Premium features should add value, not gate core functionality**
4. **Pricing must be simple and transparent** — no hidden fees

---

## Learner Pricing

### Free Tier (always free)
- Create a profile
- Calculate APS
- Browse matching programmes (limited results)
- Upload up to 5 documents
- Track up to 3 applications

### FundiLink Plus (freemium / future consideration)
Possible paid tier in Phase 4+. Decision pending user research.
Potential features:
- Unlimited programme matching
- Unlimited document storage
- Priority support
- Document certification guidance
- CV builder

**Price:** R49–R99/month (placeholder; needs market validation)
**Note:** Any paid tier must include a free path for learners who cannot afford it (bursary code, school sponsorship).

---

## School Pricing

### Free Pilot Tier
- Available for founding pilot schools
- Full school dashboard access
- Valid for one pilot period

### School Standard Plan (Phase 4+)
- Full school dashboard
- Unlimited learner tracking
- Usage reports
- Priority support

**Price:** R500–R1,500/month per school (placeholder; needs market validation)
**Billed:** Annually
**Discount:** Public schools in under-resourced areas — needs subsidy model

---

## Institutional Listings (Universities, TVETs)

### Free Listing
- Basic programme listing from public data
- Standard placement in search results

### Featured / Verified Listing (Phase 3+)
- Verified institution badge
- Enhanced programme profiles
- Featured placement in matching results
- Application portal direct link
- Enquiry/lead data (aggregate, POPIA-compliant)

**Price:** R2,000–R10,000/month per institution (placeholder)
**Billed:** Annually

---

## Bursary Partner Listings (Phase 6+)

### Free Listing
- Public bursary information listed in database

### Featured Bursary Listing
- Featured placement in bursary matching
- Verified funder badge
- Matched applicant reach metrics (anonymised)

**Price:** R3,000–R15,000/month (placeholder)

License FundiLink technology to NGOs, corporates, or provincial education departments.

## Pricing Validation Plan

Before finalising prices:
1. Survey learners on willingness to pay for premium features
2. Interview school principals on school budget availability
3. Interview university admissions offices on listing value
4. Research comparable EdTech pricing in SA and emerging markets
5. Consider impact investor/grant funding to subsidise learner access
