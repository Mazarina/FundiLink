# 09 — Brand Identity

## Title
FundiLink by ZulTek — Brand Identity Guidelines

## Purpose
Define FundiLink's brand identity so that all product, marketing, and communication materials are consistent.

---

## Brand Names

| Element | Value |
|---|---|
| Company | ZulTek |

| Full product name | FundiLink by ZulTek |

| Tagline | One profile. Every opportunity. |

## Name Explanation
- **Fundi** — from isiZulu/isiXhosa, meaning "learner" or "one who learns/studies" — deeply rooted in South African language and culture
- **Link** — connecting learners to opportunities
- Together: FundiLink = the link between learners and their futures

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Clear and simple** — no jargon; write like you are speaking to a 17-year-old from a rural school
- **Encouraging** — celebrate progress; every step forward matters
- **Honest** — never promise what we can't deliver; never overstate our role
- **Action-oriented** — tell learners what to do next
- **Warm but professional** — friendly, not patronising

### Example Messages

Good: "Your APS score is 28. Here are 12 programmes you qualify for."
Bad: "Based on your academic performance metrics, the following programmes meet your eligibility criteria."

Good: "Upload your matric results to complete your document checklist."
Bad: "Please ensure your matriculation certificate has been uploaded to the document repository."

## Visual Identity (Placeholder)

The following are design direction notes. A professional brand designer should produce the final visual identity.

| Element | Direction |
|---|---|
| Primary colour | Deep blue or teal — trust, education, opportunity |
| Accent colour | Warm orange or gold — energy, South African warmth |
| Typography | Modern, clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins) |
| Imagery | Real South African learners; diverse, authentic, not stock-photo generic |
| Icons | Simple, clear, accessible |
| Logo | Wordmark with a subtle link or connection visual motif |

---

## Brand Don'ts

- Do not claim official government or university affiliation unless formally established
- Do not use FundiLink to mislead learners about admission outcomes
- Do not use imagery that feels foreign or non-South African
- Do not use patronising language that talks down to learners
- Do not use corporate jargon in learner-facing copy

---

## Positioning Statement

FundiLink is the platform that gives every South African learner — no matter where they come from — the information, tools, and guidance to reach their potential.
