# 06 — Pitch One-Pager

## Title
FundiLink by ZulTek — Investor and Partner Pitch One-Pager

## Purpose
A concise, compelling overview of FundiLink for investors, partners, and potential institutional collaborators.

---

## The Problem
Every year, thousands of qualifying South African learners miss university, bursaries, and skills opportunities because the process is broken. It's fragmented across dozens of portals, requires expensive data, demands guidance that most rural schools can't provide, and punishes learners who miss one deadline or submit the wrong document.

## The Solution
**FundiLink** — one profile, every opportunity.

FundiLink gives every South African learner a single intelligent platform to:
- Calculate their APS score instantly
- Discover every programme and bursary they qualify for
- Organise and track their application documents
- Track applications across multiple institutions
- Get deadline reminders so they never miss a chance

Learners do it from their phone. For free.

## The Market
- 600,000+ Grade 12 learners per year
- 26 public universities, 50 TVET colleges, hundreds of bursary programmes
- R40 billion+ in bursary and NSFAS funding annually
- 3 million+ post-school learners in the PSET system

## The Business Model
- **Institutional listings:** Universities and TVETs pay to feature their programmes
- **Bursary partner listings:** Funders pay to reach matched, eligible learners
- **School subscriptions:** Schools pay for the counsellor dashboard
- **Premium features:** Optional paid features for learners
- **Data insights:** Anonymised trend reports for institutions and government

## Why Now
- South Africa's post-school access crisis is a national priority
- Smartphone penetration is high; mobile internet is accessible
- No comprehensive, learner-first platform exists for this journey
- WhatsApp and social media create a scalable distribution channel

## The Team
ZulTek — South African technology company building FundiLink as its flagship product.

## Traction
- Product: In development (MVP: FundiLink SmartApply)
- Pilot: School partnership programme planned
- Status: Seeking early institutional partnerships and pilot school commitments

## The Ask
- **Pilots:** 5 schools for the school dashboard pilot
- **Partnerships:** Universities and TVETs for programme data and eventual integration
- **Investment:** Seed funding to complete MVP and first go-to-market phase
