## Problem Section

**Heading:** The application process is broken. We're fixing it.

**Body:**
Every year, thousands of learners who qualify for university, bursaries, and skills programmes never get there.

Not because they aren't good enough. Because the system is complicated, fragmented, and built for people who already have help.

FundiLink is built for everyone else.

## How It Works Section

**Heading:** Three steps to your future

1. **Create your profile** — Add your personal details and NSC results in minutes.
2. **See your opportunities** — FundiLink calculates your APS and matches you to qualifying programmes and funding.
3. **Track and apply** — Organise your documents, track your applications, and never miss a deadline.
