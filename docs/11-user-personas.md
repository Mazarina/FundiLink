# 11 — User Personas

## Title
FundiLink — User Personas

## Purpose
Define the primary and secondary user personas to guide product, design, and content decisions.

---

## Persona 1: Thabo — The Rural Grade 12 Learner

**Age:** 18
**Location:** Small town, Limpopo
**School:** Under-resourced rural public school
**Device:** Android smartphone (data-conscious)
**Internet access:** Mobile data (limited budget); uses WhatsApp daily

**Background:**
Thabo is the first in his family who might go to university. He got a solid matric pass but doesn't know what it means in terms of what he qualifies for. His school has one guidance counsellor for 800 learners. He's heard of NSFAS but isn't sure how it works. He's excited but overwhelmed.

**Goals:**
- Find out what he qualifies for
- Get NSFAS funding so he doesn't need to ask his parents for money
- Apply to at least two universities and a backup TVET
- Not miss any deadlines

**Frustrations:**
- "I don't know where to start"
- "I filled in one application wrong and had to start again"
- "I don't have data to be opening 10 different websites"
- "My counsellor doesn't have time for everyone"

**How FundiLink Helps:**
One profile, instant APS, programme matches, document checklist, application tracker. On his phone. Simple.

## Persona 2: Lerato — The Urban Matric Learner Who Needs Funding

**Age:** 17
**Location:** Soweto, Gauteng
**School:** Township public school, better resourced than rural
**Device:** Smartphone + occasional school computer
**Internet access:** Home WiFi + mobile data

**Background:**
Lerato has good results and wants to study medicine. She knows her APS is probably good enough for a few universities but doesn't know which ones accept her subjects. Her family can't afford university fees. She needs a full bursary or NSFAS.

**Goals:**
- Find medical and health science programmes matching her APS
- Find bursaries specifically for health sciences
- Apply early to multiple institutions as backup
- Understand the funding process step by step

**Frustrations:**
- Too many websites to check for different bursaries
- Hard to know which bursaries she actually qualifies for
- Scared of applying to the wrong programme and wasting a chance

**How FundiLink Helps:**
APS calculator confirms her eligibility; programme matching shows her options; bursary matching (Phase 6) connects her to funding; document vault keeps everything organised.

## Persona 3: Mrs Dlamini — The Guidance Counsellor

**Age:** 45
**Location:** KwaZulu-Natal, medium-sized town
**Role:** Guidance counsellor and Life Orientation teacher
**Device:** School laptop and personal smartphone
**Learners she supports:** ~400 Grade 12 learners

**Background:**
Mrs Dlamini genuinely cares about her learners but is overwhelmed. Application season is chaos. She helps learners one by one, often after hours. She manually tracks who has applied where on a spreadsheet. She misses learners who need help but don't ask.

**Goals:**
- See which learners are on track and which need help
- Reduce the manual work of tracking applications
- Help learners get the right documents together
- Feel confident her learners aren't missing deadlines

**How FundiLink Helps:**
School dashboard shows her all her learners' progress. She can see who hasn't uploaded documents, who's missing deadlines, and flag learners who need support — without manual spreadsheets.

---

## Persona 4: Sipho — The Support Agent

**Age:** 26
**Location:** Remote (work-from-home)
**Role:** FundiLink Support Agent
**Goal:** Help learners who are stuck or confused complete their profiles and applications

**Background:**
Sipho handles inbound support queries from learners. He needs to quickly find a learner's profile, understand where they are in the process, and help them take the next step.

**How FundiLink Helps:**
Admin portal search lets him pull up a learner's profile, see their documents and applications, and add notes — all with a full audit trail.

---

## Persona 5: Admin — The Platform Manager

**Role:** FundiLink Admin (ZulTek operations staff)
**Goal:** Keep the programme database up to date, manage users, run reports, support school onboarding

**How FundiLink Helps:**
Admin portal with full CRUD for institutions and programmes, user management, and usage reports.
