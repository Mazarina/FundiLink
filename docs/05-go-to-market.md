# 05 — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Title
FundiLink — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Purpose
Define how FundiLink will reach, acquire, and retain learners and school partners.

---

## Target Segments

### Primary: Grade 12 Learners
- 600,000+ Grade 12 learners per year nationally
- Priority: rural and semi-urban, under-resourced schools
- Mobile-first users, WhatsApp-native
- Often first-generation applicants with limited guidance

### Secondary: Guidance Counsellors and Schools
- School counsellors and teachers helping learners with applications
- School management wanting visibility into learner outcomes
- Key channel partners for learner acquisition

### Tertiary: Universities, TVETs, and Bursary Providers
- Institutions wanting to reach qualified learners
- Funders wanting efficient, matched access to eligible applicants

---

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: School Pilot Programme
- Identify 3–5 pilot schools (mix of rural and urban, different provinces)
- Partner with guidance counsellors to introduce FundiLink in class
- Use feedback to refine the product before wider launch
- Build case studies and testimonials

### Phase 2: Organic and Community Growth
- WhatsApp group sharing (learners share with peers)
- Social media: TikTok and Instagram targeting Grade 12 learners
- School holiday workshops (university application workshops)
- Partner with NGOs and student support organisations

### Phase 3: Institutional Partnerships
- Approach universities and TVETs to list their programmes officially
- Approach bursary providers for featured listings
- Approach DHET for potential endorsement or partnership

---

## Key Messages

**For learners:**
"Don't miss your chance. FundiLink helps you find what you qualify for, get your documents ready, and track your applications — all in one place. Free."

**For schools:**
"Give your learners the support they deserve. FundiLink helps your Grade 12 class navigate university applications without overwhelming your counsellors."

**For institutions:**
"Reach the learners who qualify for your programmes. FundiLink matches them to you."

---

## Channels

| Channel | Audience | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| School partnerships | Learners via counsellors | High (MVP) |
| WhatsApp | Learners | High (Phase 5) |
| TikTok / Instagram | Learners | Medium |
| NGO partnerships | Rural learners | Medium |
| University partnerships | Institutions | Medium |
| PR / Media | Broad awareness | Low (post-MVP) |

---

## Pricing for Learners
Free for all learner-facing features in MVP. Premium features to be considered in Phase 4.
