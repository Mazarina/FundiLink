# 01 — Product Vision

## Title
FundiLink by ZulTek — Product Vision

## Purpose
Define the long-term vision, mission, and strategic intent of FundiLink.

---

## Vision Statement
Every South African learner, regardless of where they grow up or how resourced their school is, has equal access to the opportunities they deserve.

## Mission Statement
FundiLink gives every learner one intelligent profile that connects them to universities, TVET colleges, bursaries, skills programmes, and early career opportunities — removing the complexity, cost, and confusion that prevents qualified learners from realising their potential.

## Problem Statement
South Africa produces hundreds of thousands of matric graduates each year. Many qualify for higher education and bursary funding, yet never access it. The root causes include:
- Fragmented application processes across dozens of portals
- Limited access to qualified guidance counsellors, especially in rural areas
- Lack of awareness about qualifying programmes and funding
- Document preparation complexity causing preventable rejections
- Digital access barriers for mobile-first users
- No single source of truth for the learner's journey

## The Opportunity
South Africa's post-school education and training (PSET) system is large, well-funded, and motivated to increase access. The gap is not funding — it is navigation. FundiLink fills that gap.

## Product Vision
FundiLink becomes the platform every South African learner uses from their first look at tertiary education through to their first career opportunity. It is:
- **Personal** — one profile, intelligently personalised
- **Accessible** — mobile-first, designed for low-bandwidth environments
- **Trustworthy** — transparent about what it is and what it is not
- **Empowering** — gives learners agency and information, not dependency

## Strategic Differentiators
1. Rural and mobile-first design (competitors are desktop-centric)
2. APS calculator + programme matching in one place
3. Document vault that reduces rejection risk
4. WhatsApp integration (future) — meeting learners where they are
5. School counsellor integration — bringing FundiLink into the school
6. Privacy-first, POPIA-compliant design
