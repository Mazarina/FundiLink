## Slide 2: The Problem
Every year in South Africa:
- 600,000+ learners write matric
- Thousands qualify for university but don't apply correctly
- Thousands miss bursaries they are eligible for
- The application process is fragmented, confusing, and punishing

**Root cause:** No single, accessible platform exists for the learner journey.
**Who suffers most:** Rural learners, first-generation applicants, under-resourced schools.

---

## Slide 3: The Cost of Inaction
- SA's skills shortage costs the economy billions annually
- Unfilled university places despite qualified applicants
- NSFAS funding goes unclaimed due to application failures
- Generational poverty cycles continue when learners don't access higher education

---

## Slide 4: The Solution
**FundiLink SmartApply**
One profile. Every opportunity.

- APS calculator — know your score instantly
- Programme matching — discover what you qualify for
- Document vault — never lose a document again
- Application tracker — never miss a deadline
- School dashboard — counsellors support learners at scale

All on a smartphone. Free for learners.
